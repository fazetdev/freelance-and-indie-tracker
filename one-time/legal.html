Business Registration

This step gives you legal standing and can make you appear more professional to clients. While not strictly necessary for an entry-level freelancer, it's a valuable long-term goal.

Client Contract Template

A simple contract is a crucial tool for protecting your work. It clearly outlines the terms of your engagement and prevents misunderstandings.

Secure Business Insurance

As you grow and take on larger projects, professional indemnity insurance can protect you against claims of negligence or mistakes in your work.
